      Our Background History

      Insert your company's background history here. You can add relevant information about your company's journey, mission, and values.

      In the bustling city of Ipoh, a unique café named the Artisan Bakehouse came to life on the 27th of July, 2020. Driven by a passion for wholesome and healthy desserts, this café has been dedicated to serving delectable gluten-free and nut-free pastries, bakeries, and beverages. This is the story of how the Artisan Bakehouse carved its niche in the culinary world, providing a haven for those seeking nourishing treats without compromising on taste.

      The idea of the Artisan Bakehouse was born from the personal journey of its founder, Ms. Amni Mazali. After struggling with dietary restrictions and food allergies for years, she realized the scarcity of safe and delicious dessert options for people with similar dietary needs in Ipoh. Determined to make a positive change, Ms. Amni envisioned a café that catered to the gluten-free and nut-free community while promoting healthy and mindful eating.

      From hearty loaves of bread to delicate pastries and cakes, every item was crafted with precision and passion. The menu catered not only to those with dietary restrictions but also to health-conscious individuals who sought out guilt-free treats. The Bakehouse also served a variety of beverages, including specialty coffees, organic teas, and fresh fruit juices. The café sourced its ingredients from local suppliers, ensuring that only the finest, freshest produce made its way into the kitchen.
